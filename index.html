Welcome To The 4YP

My name is Realeboha Mofube, I have 21 years of age. I was born and raised in Leribe, Hlotse. I studied my preschool, primary and partially high school. From there I moved to Maseru district, And I completed my highschool at Maseru Private Academy in 2021.

My Journey

Growing up, I always had interest in gadgets and tech related stuff. So the next year after I completed highschool, I enrolled into Limkokwing University of creative Technology majoring in Business IT. I thought this would be the beggining of a life changing journey, indeed it was but not how I had actually expected. Everything was going okay for the first semester at time went we got introduced to a module called Calculus which for the first time we failed as the whole class, but we were given a second chance and some made it but me and a few others didnt make it still. That is where the 4YP(4 year plan) startted, i kept on trying but kept failing this module because of silly careless mistakes. Eventually I started losing hope, hanging with bad friends, and making bad descions and my grades reflected it. Until I decided it was enough! I had to seek help, but first I figured the first step should be a chamge in senerio. Had to go back to the drawing board and restrategize. Thats when I found out about Holberton school on facebook and I started doing my research about the school and even organized a meeting with one of the lectures there who told me all about the school and I just kept falling in love with the school.I am a hard working individual with a vision of utalizing Technology to create jobs for the youth of my country or atleast openening their minds about the struggles of school, friends and temptations.I DO NOT RGRET MY MISTAKES AND FALL BACKS FOR I USE THEM TO TEACH OTHERS. MY MISTAKES ARE LESSONS THAT SHAPED ME, TURNING WHAT ONCE FELT LIKE WASTED TIME INTO THE FOUNDATION OF A BETTER LIFE.
